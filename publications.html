Due to webscraping issues with my email address, Google Scholar and many other publication venues incorrectly parse my last name as “De Badyn” or “de Badyn”, instead of “Hudoba de Badyn”. If you are going to cite my papers, kindly make sure that the citation key is corrected. I have prepared a biblatex file with my name corrected here using the BetterBibtex extension for Zotero, for your convenience.

Research

My research focuses on the control and estimation of networked dynamical systems, with an emphasis on sustainable infrastructure. Together with my group and collaborators, I work on distributed and optimal control, smart buildings and district energy systems, digital twins and data-enabled control, spacecraft guidance, navigation and control, and energy communities / virtual power plants.
